Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Information We Collect

CalcVerse is designed to respect your privacy. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your calculation data to any server.

The only data stored is in your browser's localStorage and includes:

  • Your theme preference (dark/light mode)
  • Your favorite calculators
  • Calculation history (stored locally only)

Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography. When you load CalcVerse, your browser may request font files from Google's servers. No personal data is shared through this process.

Cookies

CalcVerse does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. All preferences are stored using the browser's localStorage API, which does not transmit data to servers.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via our about page.
