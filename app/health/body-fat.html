How it's calculated

US Navy Method: Uses circumference measurements and height. Men: 86×log₁₀(waist-neck) − 70×log₁₀(height) + 36.76. Women: 163×log₁₀(waist+hip-neck) − 97×log₁₀(height) − 78.39.
