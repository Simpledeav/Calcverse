Heart Rate Zones

Find your target heart rate zones. Enter your age to see personalized fat burn, cardio, vigorous, and peak heart rate ranges.

Zone Breakdown

💡 Tip: For fat loss, stay in the Fat Burn zone (50-60% of max HR). For cardiovascular fitness, aim for Cardio (60-70%) or Vigorous (70-85%) zones.

How it's calculated

Max HR = 220 − age. Zones are percentages of max HR: Rest (<50%), Fat Burn (50-60%), Cardio (60-70%), Vigorous (70-85%), Peak (85-100%).
