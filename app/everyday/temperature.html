How temperature conversion works

°C → °F: (°C × 9/5) + 32

°F → °C: (°F − 32) × 5/9

°C → K: °C + 273.15

K → °C: K − 273.15

°F → K: (°F + 459.67) × 5/9

K → °F: K × 9/5 − 459.67

°R → K: °R × 5/9

Formulas use Kelvin as the base unit, converting through K for all pairs.
