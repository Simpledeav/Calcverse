Chemistry Reference

Molar Mass

Sum of atomic masses × subscript counts.

M = n / V

Molarity = moles / volume (liters)

C₁V₁ = C₂V₂

Dilution: M₁V₁ = M₂V₂

PV = nRT

R = 0.08206 L·atm/mol·K

How it's calculated

Molar Mass: Parses the chemical formula, sums atomic masses from the periodic table.

Molarity: M = n / V. Enter any 2 to solve for the third.

Dilution: C₁V₁ = C₂V₂. Enter any 3 to solve for the fourth.

Ideal Gas: PV = nRT with R = 0.08206 L·atm·mol⁻¹·K⁻¹.
