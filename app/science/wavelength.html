Visible Spectrum

Conversion

1 eV = 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ J

How it's calculated

Wave Equation: λ = c/f, f = c/λ. Speed of light c = 2.998×10⁸ m/s.

Photon Energy: E = hf = hc/λ. Planck's constant h = 6.626×10⁻³⁴ J·s. 1 eV = 1.602×10⁻¹⁹ J.
