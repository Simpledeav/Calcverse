How it's calculated

Boyle's Law: P₁V₁ = P₂V₂ (constant T). Charles's Law: V₁/T₁ = V₂/T₂ (constant P).

Combined Gas Law: P₁V₁/T₁ = P₂V₂/T₂.

Ideal Gas Law: PV = nRT. R = 0.08206 L·atm·mol⁻¹·K⁻¹.
