How it's calculated

Kinetic Energy: KE = ½mv² (Joules). Potential Energy: PE = mgh (J).

Work: W = Fd (Joules). Power: P = W/t (Watts).

1 Joule = 1 N·m = 1 kg·m²/s². 1 Watt = 1 J/s.
