Percentage Calculator

Calculate percentages in four modes: percent of a number, percent of total, percentage change, and increase/decrease.

Quick Reference

X% of Y

Result = (X / 100) × Y

X is what % of Y?

Result = (X / Y) × 100%

% Change

((New - Old) / Old) × 100%

+ / - %

Original × (1 + Change%/100)

Tip

For tip: 15% of $127 = $19.05. Each person (4): $36.51

How it's calculated

All percentage calculations follow standard arithmetic formulas. The engine handles edge cases like division by zero and very large numbers gracefully.
