How it's calculated

This calculator evaluates arithmetic expressions following the standard order of operations:

  1. Parentheses — expressions inside ( ) are evaluated first
  2. Exponents — ^ (power) and √ (root)
  3. Multiplication & Division — × and ÷, left to right
  4. Addition & Subtraction — + and −, left to right

The engine uses the shunting-yard algorithm for safe, eval()-free evaluation.
