How it's calculated

Add: a/b + c/d = (ad + bc) / bd
Subtract: a/b − c/d = (ad − bc) / bd
Multiply: a/b × c/d = ac / bd
Divide: a/b ÷ c/d = ad / bc

All results are simplified to lowest terms using GCD.
