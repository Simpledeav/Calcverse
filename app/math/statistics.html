How it's calculated

Mean: μ = Σx / n
Variance (pop): σ² = Σ(x − μ)² / n
Variance (sample): s² = Σ(x − μ)² / (n − 1)
Std Dev: σ = √variance
Quartiles: Q1 = 25th percentile, Q2 = median, Q3 = 75th percentile
