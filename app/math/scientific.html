How it's calculated

This calculator evaluates expressions using the shunting-yard algorithm with full operator precedence:

  1. Parentheses — expressions inside ( ) are evaluated first
  2. Functions — sin, cos, tan, log, ln, √, and their inverses
  3. Exponents — ^ (power), √ (root), ! (factorial)
  4. Multiplication & Division — × and ÷, left to right
  5. Addition & Subtraction — + and −, left to right

In DEG mode, trigonometric functions convert degrees to radians internally. Common constants π and e are recognized as named values.
